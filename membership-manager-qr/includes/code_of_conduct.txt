## Conduct In Club and Online ##

* No abuse to staff or other members will be tolerated
* No discrimination will be tolerated

## Consent Is Mandatory — Always ##

* Explicit, verbal consent is required before any touch.
* No means no. Maybe means no. Silence is not consent.
* Consent can be withdrawn at any time — immediately and without question.
* Prior consent does not apply to future interactions.
* Respect personal boundaries without negotiation or pressure.

## No Means No — No Exceptions ##

* Rejection must be accepted politely and immediately.
* No persuasion, sulking, intimidation, or repeated asking.
* Members who ignore boundaries will be removed.

## Respect for All Members ##

* No harassment, discrimination, or unwanted comments about body, race, gender identity, sexuality, age, or appearance
* No shaming of preferences, limits, or participation level
* No aggressive behavior, posturing, or entitlement

## Privacy & Discretion ##

* No photos or videos anywhere in the venue
* Phones must remain in designated areas only (if applicable)
* Do not share names, personal details, or identities outside the club
* What happens here stays here

## Hygiene & Cleanliness ##

* Members must arrive clean and groomed
* Safe sex is strongly suggested (condoms provided)
* Dispose of used protection and personal items properly (not in toilets)
* Respect shared spaces and clean up after yourself

## Substance Use Policy ##

* No illegal drugs on premises
* Excessive intoxication will result in removal
* Anyone visibly impaired cannot consent and will not be permitted to participate

## Dress Code ##

* Try to look your best - clean, casual, and your sexy self
* Respect the tone and theme of the event
* Appropriate attire required in common areas unless otherwise designated

## Playroom Etiquette ##

* Observe before joining - never assume participation is welcome
* Always ask all parties before entering a scene
* No crowding or hovering
* Respect designated private areas

## Couples & Singles Policy ##

* Follow the club's admission guidelines
* No circumventing entry rules
* Respect ratio policies and event-specific rules

## Conflict Resolution ##

* Any member feeling uncomfortable should notify staff immediately
* Staff decisions are final
* Violations may result in warnings, suspension, or permanent ban

## Zero Tolerance Policy ##

Immediate removal for:

* Non-consensual touching
* Harassment or coercion
* Filming or photography (or any recording device including a phone in play areas)
* Aggression or threatening behavior
* Violating privacy rules

## Community Values ##

This club is built on:

* Consent
* Respect
* Communication
* Discretion
* Inclusivity
* Mutual enjoyment
